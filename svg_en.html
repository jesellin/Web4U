Basic SVG

Scalable vector container inside HTML.

<svg width="200" height="80"></svg>

Practical use: Graphics that do not pixelate when scaling. W3Schools

Shapes

Rectangles, circles, and ellipses are basic.

<circle cx="40" cy="40" r="30" />

Practical use: Simple diagrams and decorations. W3Schools

Paths

Allow complex shapes using commands.

<path d="M10 10 H 90 V 70 H 10 Z" />

Practical use: Logos and custom paths. W3Schools

Text in SVG

Text that can be scaled and transformed.

<text x="10" y="20">Hello SVG</text>

Practical use: Labels in charts and posters. W3Schools

Icons and symbols

Reuse symbols with <use> or sprites.

<symbol id="s1">...</symbol> <use href="#s1"/>

Practical use: Custom icons, indicators, and logos. W3Schools
